Crystals Collector

Instructions to Play

  • Randomly selected number are presented to you at the start of the game.
  • Each crystal has hidden value which will be displayed after the click.
  • Click crystals to match your points to the random number given to you at the beginning.
  • The value of each crystal changes each game.

If you are able to match your total score with randomly displayed number, you will win. Otherwise, you will lose and game will reset.
